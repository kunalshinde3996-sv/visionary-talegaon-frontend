Our Mission & Vision

Creating Bright Futures for Special Children

At Visionary Supermind School, we believe every child deserves a chance to shine. Our structured and personalized programs help children with Autism, ADHD, and Learning Disabilities grow with confidence and independence.

We focus on communication skills, social interaction, reading, writing, language development, daily living skills, self-care, fine motor skills, dressing, eating habits, domestic skills, and behavioral support.

Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups do you accept?
We accept children from 2 to 18 years of age. Our programs are tailored according to each child's developmental level and individual needs.
What conditions do you specialize in?
We specialize in Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD), ADHD, Learning Disabilities, speech and communication delays, and daily living skill development.
How do I enroll my child?
Simply fill out the contact form on this page or call us at +91 9665065499. We will schedule a free initial consultation to understand your child's needs and suggest the best program.
Do you offer home visits or online sessions?
Currently we offer in-person sessions at our center in Bhosari, Pune. Please contact us to know about any special arrangements.
What are your school timings?
We are open Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM to 7:00 PM. You can visit us or call during these hours for any queries.
Is there a free consultation available?
Yes! We offer a free initial consultation for all new families. Contact us to book your visit and we will guide you through the best program for your child.
